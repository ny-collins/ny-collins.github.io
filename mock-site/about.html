About Swiftel Fibre

Your trusted partner for high-speed, reliable fibre internet in Kenya.

Connecting Kenya, One Home and Business at a Time

Swiftel Fibre is a leading internet service provider dedicated to bringing state-of-the-art fibre optic connectivity to homes and businesses across Kenya. Founded on the principles of reliability, affordability, and exceptional customer service, we are committed to bridging the digital divide and empowering communities with seamless access to the online world. We believe that everyone deserves fast, stable, and affordable internet. That's why we've invested in building a robust, future-proof fibre network designed to meet the ever-growing demands of today's digital landscape.

Our Mission

To provide unparalleled fibre internet services that enable individuals and businesses to thrive in the digital age, fostering innovation, communication, and economic growth across Kenya.

Our Vision

To be the leading and most trusted fibre internet provider in Kenya, recognized for our commitment to technological excellence, customer satisfaction, and community empowerment.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do and how we serve you.

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency, and a strong ethical compass in all our dealings.

Innovation

We continuously seek and implement cutting-edge technologies to enhance our services and deliver the best.

Customer-Centricity

Our customers are at the heart of everything. We are dedicated to providing outstanding support and solutions.

Reliability

We are committed to delivering consistent, stable, and dependable internet connectivity you can always count on.
