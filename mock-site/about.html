About Swiftel Fibre

Founded in March 2021, Swiftel Fibre (formerly Swift Fibre) is a digital enabler dedicated to delivering stable, fast, and affordable internet to homes and businesses across Nairobi — and beyond.

From our humble beginnings, we’ve grown into one of the fastest-rising ISPs in the region. We combine technical expertise with unmatched customer service to make connectivity seamless and accessible.

Currently, we serve clients across Kasarani and Njiru Constituencies — but we’re just getting started. Our mission is to expand into even more neighborhoods as our infrastructure and partnerships grow.

  • Licensed ISP with direct peering and global transit
  • Tailored packages for homes, estates, and businesses
  • Revenue-sharing options for estate managers and agents
  • Dedicated local support team and rapid installations
  • Uptime commitment of 99.9% backed by SLAs
